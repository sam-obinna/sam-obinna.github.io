Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our automation services

What types of processes can you automate? +

We can automate virtually any repetitive business process including data entry, email management, lead routing, invoice processing, social media posting, inventory management, customer onboarding, report generation, and much more. If it involves moving data between systems or performing the same steps repeatedly, we can likely automate it.

How quickly can automation be implemented? +

Simple automations can be deployed within 1-2 weeks, while complex multi-system integrations typically take 4-8 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during our discovery phase and keep you updated throughout the implementation process.

Do I need technical knowledge to use the automations? +

Not at all! We design our automations to be user-friendly and provide comprehensive training. Most automations run completely in the background, and any user interfaces we create are intuitive and easy to use. We also provide ongoing support and documentation.

What if my current software isn't supported? +

We work with hundreds of business applications and can create custom integrations for virtually any system with an API. If your software doesn't have an API, we can often find alternative solutions using web automation, database connections, or file-based integration methods.

How much do automation projects typically cost? +

Costs vary based on complexity and scope. Simple automations start around $2,500, while comprehensive enterprise solutions can range from $15,000-$50,000+. Most clients see ROI within 3-6 months through time savings and error reduction. We provide detailed cost estimates after understanding your specific needs.

What kind of ongoing support do you provide? +

We offer comprehensive ongoing support including monitoring, maintenance, updates, and troubleshooting. Our support packages range from basic monitoring to full managed services. We also provide training sessions and documentation to help your team get the most out of their automations.
