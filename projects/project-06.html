Case Study 06

US Music Festival Company

Music & Entertainment • 1 month

Timeline
1 month
Role
Solo build
Tech Stack
🔧Make.com'">Make.com
🔧Soundchart'">Soundchart
🔧Airtable'">Airtable

Problem

Identifying fast-rising electronic artists for booking opportunities. Manual monitoring was slow and inconsistent, missing emerging talent before competitors could secure them for festival lineups.

Solution

Make.com workflow pulling 3,000-5,000 artists weekly from Soundcharts API. Filtered by electronic genre + platform criteria (Spotify 2K-500K followers, Instagram 2K-50K). Excluded 80+ unwanted genres. Weighted momentum scoring (Instagram 20%, TikTok 40%, Spotify 40%) with bonus for Spotify editorial playlists. Filtered for artists debuting within 3 years. Top 50 output to Airtable with weekly reports.
