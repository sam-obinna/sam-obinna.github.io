Problem

Small team manually processing 400+ documents monthly (50-80 lease agreements, 80-100 inspection notices, 150+ contractor documents). Drafting, formatting, sending by hand. Managing 500+ apartments with manual workflows.

Solution

End-to-end document automation suite via Zapier: LeadSimple pulls tenant/property data, ChatGPT validates/deduplicates, DocuSign auto-generates and sends 8 document types (Lease Renewal, 60-Day Non-Renewal, Intent to Lease, 14-Day Pay or Vacate, 10-Day Comply, Hand-Delivered Notices, Two-Day Notice to Enter, Tenant Data Validation Module).
