Problem

Complex clinical research study requiring automated participant enrollment, survey distribution, data collection, and engagement monitoring for mental health research with strict HIPAA compliance requirements.

Solution

Built comprehensive Make.com scenario suite: automated enrollment intake, eligibility routing, survey window scheduling, reminder cascades, completion tracking, payment processing. Integrated Typeform for data collection, Airtable as database, Metabase/Mixpanel for analytics, with automated participant communication workflows.
