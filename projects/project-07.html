The Problem

Manual order entry between ClickFunnels and Shopify was consuming significant staff time that should have been directed at ocean cleanup operations. Environmental workers were spending hours each week on data entry instead of mission-critical fieldwork - a painful misalignment of people and purpose for a cause-driven organisation.

⚙️

The Solution

Implemented a Zapier automation that connects ClickFunnels purchases directly to Shopify - automatically creating orders and preparing them for fulfilment the moment a purchase completes. Every sale now flows instantly from the sales funnel to the fulfilment system with zero human involvement, freeing the team to focus entirely on their environmental mission.
