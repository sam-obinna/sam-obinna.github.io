Problem

Manual order entry between ClickFunnels and Shopify consuming staff time that should be focused on ocean cleanup operations. Environmental workers spending hours on data entry instead of mission-critical work.

Solution

Zapier automation connecting ClickFunnels purchases to auto-create Shopify orders and prep for fulfillment, eliminating all manual order processing. Every purchase instantly flows from sales funnel to fulfillment system.
