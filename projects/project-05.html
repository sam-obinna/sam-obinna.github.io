Case Study 05

UK Motorcycle Storage Company

Hospitality / Storage • 2 weeks

Role

🔧Airtable'">Airtable

Problem

No operational infrastructure in place. Needed full client lifecycle management (prospecting to onboarding to bike tracking to offboarding) before launch. Launch was 8 weeks away with zero systems.

Solution

End-to-end CRM and operations system in Airtable with QR code-based bike identification, automated contract generation via Microsoft Word, invoice creation through Xero integration, and customer-facing booking portal built on Softr. Make.com orchestrated workflows across all platforms.
