Danish Specialty Coffee Company

E-commerce / Food & Beverage • 5 weeks

Timeline

🔧Pipedrive'">Pipedrive

Problem

Manual data sync between CRM, email marketing, and order management. Customer records updated manually, campaign segmentation done in spreadsheets, no unified view of B2B (400+ business clients) vs D2C customers.

Solution

Make.com automation connecting Pipedrive to Klaviyo to Airtable to Google Sheets. Automated customer segmentation (B2B vs D2C), order sync across platforms, triggered email campaigns based on purchase behavior, unified reporting dashboard.
