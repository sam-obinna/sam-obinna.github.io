The Problem

The agency was dealing with excessive manual post-meeting work - team members had to manually upload Fireflies transcripts to the right client folders in ClickUp after every call. Onboarding new team members onto client accounts took far too long, and building pipelines for each new insurance company client meant starting from scratch every time.

⚙️

The Solution

Delivered a multi-part internal operations automation: (1) Fireflies-to-ClickUp integration that automatically posts meeting transcripts and action items to the correct client folder after every call, (2) a Slack knowledge bot via Zapier that indexes all client data for instant team queries, and (3) a reusable pipeline template system so new insurance company clients can be onboarded in days, not weeks.
