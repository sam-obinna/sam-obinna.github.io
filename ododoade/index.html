Ododoade's Breakthrough Project

Vendor Invoice Automation at Diversified USA

The Challenge

The accounting team was drowning in vendor invoices - spending 15+ hours weekly on manual processing, with frequent duplicate work and missed invoices causing vendor relationship issues.

My Solution

I built an intelligent system using Microsoft Power Automate that automatically assigns invoices based on accountant availability, sends real-time alerts, and prevents duplicate processing through smart validation rules.

The Impact

15+ hours saved weekly for the team, 100% accuracy achieved, zero duplicate work, and significantly improved vendor relationships. This was my "aha" moment about automation's potential.

Real Estate / Architecture

AI-Powered Lead Generation System

Client Challenge

A Canadian architecture firm was missing early-stage opportunities because manually monitoring universities, municipalities, and news sources was impossible to do consistently.

What I Built

Created an N8N workflow with 4 specialized AI agents using Gemini Flash 2.0 to automatically extract project data from PDFs, websites, and documents, all feeding into a centralized Airtable system.

Results Achieved

90% of sources now monitored automatically, 85% of leads match their criteria, and they're finding opportunities 6-12 months before competitors - all at just $0.45 per workflow run.
