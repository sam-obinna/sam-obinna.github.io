Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our automation services

You're a newer agency - why should I choose OpSynx? +

Great question! Being newer means we're hungrier, more attentive, and treat every client like they're our only client. We don't have 100 projects running simultaneously - you'll work directly with founders who have years of automation experience. Plus, we offer money-back guarantees and flexible terms that bigger agencies can't match.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work? +

We offer a simple money-back guarantee. If we deliver something that doesn't work as specified, we'll fix it free of charge or refund your money. No questions, no hassle. We also include a 30-day support period after launch to ensure everything runs smoothly.

What types of businesses benefit most from automation? +

Any business with repetitive tasks, data processing needs, or customer interactions can benefit from automation. We've helped companies from 10 to 10,000+ employees across industries including e-commerce, healthcare, finance, real estate, and professional services.

How long does implementation typically take? +

Implementation timelines vary based on complexity. Simple automations can be deployed in 1-2 weeks, while comprehensive enterprise solutions typically take 4-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during our discovery phase.

What platforms and tools do you work with? +

We're experts in Zapier, Make.com, N8N, Airtable, and custom API integrations. We also work with major platforms like Salesforce, HubSpot, Monday.com, Shopify, and can integrate virtually any tool with an API.

Do you provide ongoing support after implementation? +

Yes! We offer comprehensive support packages including monitoring, monthly optimization reviews, and on-demand assistance. Your automation continues to evolve and improve with our ongoing support. First 30 days of support are included with every project.

Do you require long-term contracts? +

Nope! We work on a project basis with optional ongoing support. You can cancel support services anytime with 30 days notice. We earn your business with results, not contracts.

How do you ensure data security and compliance? +

We follow enterprise-grade security protocols including encryption, secure API connections, and compliance with GDPR, SOC 2, and industry-specific regulations. All automations are built with security and privacy as top priorities.
