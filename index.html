Frequently Asked Questions

What types of automation projects do you specialize in? +

I specialize in business process automation, including CRM systems, lead generation, HR workflows, e-commerce operations, financial reporting, and AI-powered customer support. I work with platforms like n8n, Zapier, Make.com, and custom API integrations.

How long does a typical automation project take? +

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple automations can be completed in 1-2 weeks, while comprehensive systems typically take 4-8 weeks. I provide detailed timelines after understanding your specific requirements.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion? +

Yes, I offer comprehensive support packages including 24/7 monitoring, regular maintenance, updates, and training for your team. I ensure your automations continue running smoothly and evolve with your business needs.

Can you integrate with our existing systems? +

Absolutely! I specialize in integrating with existing tools and platforms. Whether it's CRMs, ERPs, databases, or custom software, I can create seamless connections to ensure smooth data flow across your entire tech stack.

What's your pricing structure? +

I offer flexible pricing based on project scope and complexity. Options include fixed-price projects, hourly consulting, and monthly retainers for ongoing work. I provide detailed quotes after our initial consultation.

How do you ensure data security in your automations? +

Security is a top priority. I implement industry-standard encryption, secure API connections, access controls, and regular security audits. All automations are built with GDPR and data protection compliance in mind.
